<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Introduction">Introduction</span></h2>
<p>The DisNCord Wiki exists to document projects undertaken by community members, and avoid the usual problem of such discussions being lost on Discord and un-indexable. It's hope for this to be a central record for everything we've done together as a group, as well as a lotus for various interests and more. Editing is open to all, although you need an account, and be verified by an admin to upload images. See <a href="/index.php?title=How_Do_I_Get_Involved&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="How Do I Get Involved (page does not exist)">How Do I Get Involved</a> for more information.
</p>
